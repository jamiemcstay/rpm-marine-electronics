Our Services

RPM Marine Electronics provides reliable, professional, and affordable boat electronics services across Ireland

083 315 6884

Based in Dun Laoghaire, RPM Marine Electronics specializes in providing marine electronics services for boat owners across Ireland. With over 10 years of experience, we have built a strong reputation for delivering high-quality and dependable marine electronics solutions. Our team of fully qualified and highly skilled technicians covers all aspects of boat electronics, including navigation systems, communication equipment, and electrical system installations. We proudly serve areas including Baldoyle, Bayside, Howth, Sutton, Malahide, Clontarf, Fairview, Swords, Artane, Blanchardstown, and the greater Dublin area. Irish Marine Electronics is fully certified and insured to carry out all types of marine electronics installations and services. For more information on our marine services, please contact us.

Navigation systems

Power Systems

Offering dependable power systems for marine vessels, we ensure optimal performance and safety with our expert installation and maintenance services.
